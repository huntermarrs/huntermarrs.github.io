Getting to Know NYC's Mother Pigeon

An interview and digital zine by Sophia Bevacqua

The easy flowing design and color pallete directly correlate to Mother Pigeon's art aesthetic. With her being as lighthearted as she is, this site encapsulates her spirit without any overdesigning.

You can easily get to know her, see some of her art, and find out her social media all in a simple scroll system.
